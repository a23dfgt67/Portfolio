The Process Behind the Work

Awesome work doesn't happen by accident. My process typically involves these key stages:

1. Discovery: Understanding the problem space and user needs through research and analysis.

2. Ideation: Brainstorming multiple solutions and creating prototypes.

3. Execution: Bringing the chosen solution to life with clean code and thoughtful design.

4. Refinement: Testing, gathering feedback, and iterating to perfection.
